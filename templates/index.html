How does VERIFACT work?

A look under the hood of our Natural Language Processing engine

1. Preprocessing

News text undergoes lowercasing, stripping of punctuation/HTML, tokenization, stopword removal, and Porter stemming to extract base semantic roots.

2. TF-IDF Weights

Word sequences are vectorized using Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency, representing articles as dense numerical feature spaces.

3. Majority Vote

Three models (Logistic Regression, Naive Bayes, PAC) analyze structural features. The consensus determines the final rating.
